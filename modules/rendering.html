Rendering

Rendering backends for SLAM tracking.

raycast_tsdf(tsdf_volume, tsdf_origin, voxel_size, T_world_camera, intrinsics, height, width, near=0.1, far=5.0, n_samples=128)[source]

Raycast a TSDF volume to render depth and normals.

Fully vectorized: all H*W rays processed simultaneously on GPU.

Parameters:

  • tsdf_volume (Tensor) – TSDF tensor [nx, ny, nz].

  • tsdf_origin (Tensor) – Origin of TSDF volume [3].

  • voxel_size (float) – Voxel size in meters.

  • T_world_camera (Tensor) – Camera pose [4, 4] (camera-to-world).

  • intrinsics (Tensor) – Camera intrinsics [3, 3] or [4, 4].

  • height (int) – Output image height.

  • width (int) – Output image width.

  • near (float) – Near plane distance in meters.

  • far (float) – Far plane distance in meters.

  • n_samples (int) – Samples along each ray.

Returns:

RenderedFrame with depth, normals, and mask.

Return type:

RenderedFrame
